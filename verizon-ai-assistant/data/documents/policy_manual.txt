Verizon Corporate Policy Manual 2025

Section 4: Installation Policies
Section 4.1: Standard professional installation is included at no cost for all new 'Pro' Fiber Plan subscriptions. This includes wiring to one primary access point. Additional wiring may incur fees. Installation appointments are scheduled within a 4-hour window.

Section 8: Return Policies
Section 8.1: Equipment must be returned within 30 days of service cancellation to avoid non-return fees. A pre-paid shipping label will be provided.
